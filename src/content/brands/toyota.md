## Why buy a used Toyota in Canada

Toyota has been the top-selling Japanese automaker in Canada for over a decade, with the RAV4 leading the SUV segment and a Toyota plant in Cambridge/Woodstock, Ontario producing RAV4 and Lexus RX domestically — making parts availability and resale liquidity exceptional.

## Key facts at a glance

- Toyota RAV4 was Canada's best-selling SUV in 2023 and 2024 with over 70,000 units delivered annually
- Toyota Canada sold approximately 245,000 vehicles in 2024, leading all Japanese brands [verify in Q3]
- Toyota's Cambridge and Woodstock, Ontario plants produce the RAV4 and Lexus RX for the North American market
