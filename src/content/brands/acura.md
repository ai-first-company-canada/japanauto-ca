## Why buy a used Acura in Canada

Acura is built by Honda at the Alliston, Ontario plant (MDX historically, plus shared platforms with the CR-V and Civic), giving Canadian luxury buyers near-Honda parts pricing and dealer access — a value position no German luxury brand can match.

## Key facts at a glance

- Acura MDX has historically been built at Honda's Alliston, Ontario plant for the North American market
- Acura SH-AWD (Super Handling All-Wheel Drive) is standard on MDX and available on RDX and TLX trims
- Acura shares engineering platforms with Honda, meaning lower long-term parts costs than German luxury rivals
