## What is AWD?

AWD is a drivetrain that powers all four wheels, distinguishing it from 2WD (front- or rear-wheel drive) and from part-time 4WD found in trucks. Modern AWD systems are typically electronically controlled and engage automatically when sensors detect wheel slip. AWD is offered across Subaru's full lineup and on most Toyota, Honda, Nissan, Mazda, Lexus, Acura, Infiniti, and Mitsubishi crossover models sold in Canada.

## Why it matters in Canada

Canadian winters across Toronto, Montreal, Calgary, and Edmonton make AWD a near-default expectation for resale value — Statistics Canada and DesRosiers Automotive Consultants both report that AWD/4WD vehicles account for the majority of light-vehicle sales in Canada, the highest mix of any G7 country. AWD does not replace winter tires, which remain the single largest factor in cold-weather stopping distance.
