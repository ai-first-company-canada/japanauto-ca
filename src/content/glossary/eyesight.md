## What is EyeSight?

EyeSight, introduced to North America in 2013, uses two windshield-mounted colour cameras (rather than radar) to perceive depth and identify pedestrians, vehicles, lane lines, and brake lights. The latest generation adds wide-angle cameras and electric brake booster integration. EyeSight is standard on most Canadian-spec Subaru Outback, Forester, Crosstrek, Impreza, Legacy, and Ascent trims.

## Why it matters in Canada

Subaru Canada makes EyeSight standard on a higher proportion of trims than most competitors, and the IIHS has repeatedly awarded EyeSight-equipped Subarus Top Safety Pick+ status — a key factor in their strong resale value among Canadian families. EyeSight performance can degrade in heavy snow, fog, or with windshield damage, so Canadian buyers should verify camera recalibration after any glass work.
