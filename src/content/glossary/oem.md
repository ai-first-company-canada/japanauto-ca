## What is OEM?

OEM parts match the vehicle's factory specification exactly in fit, materials, and tolerances. They are sold through manufacturer dealer networks (Toyota, Honda, Nissan, Mazda, Subaru, Lexus, Acura, Infiniti, Mitsubishi Canadian dealers) and contrast with aftermarket parts, which are produced by independent manufacturers and may vary in quality. OEM parts typically command a premium of 30–60% over comparable aftermarket equivalents.

## Why it matters in Canada

Canadian dealer networks honour OEM-part warranties (typically 12 months / 20,000 km) when installed by a licensed shop, and OEM parts are often required to maintain remaining manufacturer powertrain warranty coverage. For JDM-imported vehicles, OEM-Japan parts may differ in part numbers from OEM-Canada equivalents — buyers should confirm cross-reference with a dealer parts department before ordering.
