## What is VSA?

VSA licenses every motor-dealer and salesperson in BC, enforces the Motor Dealer Act and Motor Dealer Customer Compensation Fund Regulation, and administers the BC Motor Dealer Customer Compensation Fund. Salespersons must complete a VSA-approved certification course. Established in 2004, the VSA covers the Vancouver, Victoria, and Lower Mainland markets among others.

## Why it matters in Canada

Vancouver is a Tier-1 CMA and one of Canada's largest JDM-import hubs, particularly for vehicles arriving via the Port of Vancouver. Every BC dealer listing on japanauto.ca must hold a current VSA licence, and BC's Compensation Fund offers structured consumer protection comparable to OMVIC's Ontario equivalent.
