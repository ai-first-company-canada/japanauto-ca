## What is AMVIC?

AMVIC is Alberta's industry-funded regulator for the automotive sector. Established in 1999, it licenses dealers, salespeople, repair shops, vehicle inspectors, and auctioneers, and enforces the Consumer Protection Act and the Automotive Business Regulation. Every dealer and salesperson in Alberta selling used vehicles, including Japanese imports, must hold an active AMVIC licence.

## Why it matters in Canada

Alberta is the only province where the automotive industry is regulated by a delegated authority rather than a direct government body, and AMVIC compliance is mandatory for any Calgary or Edmonton dealer listing on japanauto.ca. Buyers can verify a dealer's status on amvic.org before purchase, which materially reduces fraud exposure on private-import and used-JDM transactions.
